Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping costs depend on several factors:

  • Size and weight of your package
  • Destination (domestic or international)
  • Service type (Priority Mail, Ground Advantage, etc.)
  • Additional services (insurance, tracking, etc.)

Use our Calculate Price tool to get an accurate estimate for your shipment.

You can track your package in several ways:

  1. Visit our Tracking page and enter your tracking number
  2. Use the USPS Mobile app
  3. Sign up for Informed Delivery® for automatic updates
  4. Text your tracking number to 28777 (2USPS)

Your tracking number is typically 20-22 digits long and can be found on your receipt or shipping label.

Regular Mail Delivery:

  • Monday through Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • No regular delivery on Sundays (except for Priority Mail Express)

Package Delivery:

  • Monday through Saturday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Sunday delivery available for Priority Mail Express and Amazon packages

Delivery times may vary by location and mail volume.

You can change your address online or at a Post Office:

Online (Recommended):

  1. Visit our Change of Address page
  2. Verify your identity ($1.10 fee)
  3. Complete the online form
  4. Receive confirmation

At Post Office:

  • Fill out Form 3575 (Change of Address)
  • Bring valid photo ID
  • Submit form to postal clerk

Mail forwarding typically begins within 7-10 business days.

To rent a PO Box:

  1. Find a location: Use our Post Office locator
  2. Choose a size: Small, Medium, Large, or Extra Large
  3. Bring required documents:
    • Valid photo ID
    • Proof of address (utility bill, lease, etc.)
  4. Complete application: Form 1093
  5. Pay fees: Box rental fee + key deposit

PO Box sizes and pricing vary by location. Contact your local Post Office for availability and current rates.

Current Stamp Prices (2024):

  • Forever Stamp: $0.73
  • Postcard Stamp: $0.56
  • International Letter: $1.55

About Forever Stamps:

  • Always valid for First-Class Mail, regardless of price changes
  • Can be used for letters weighing up to 1 ounce
  • Available at Post Offices, online, and many retail locations

You can buy stamps online at our Postal Store.
